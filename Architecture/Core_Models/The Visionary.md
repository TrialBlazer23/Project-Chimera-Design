##Visual & Auditory Design Model

- Role: The Visionary transforms narrative concepts into visual and auditory aesthetics.
    
- Capabilities:
    

- Concept Art Generation: Creates and refines concept art.
    
- Storyboarding: Creates detailed storyboards for visualizing scenes.
    
- AI-Assisted Pre-visualization: Uses AI tools for 3D models and animations.
    
- Soundscape & Music Composition: Designs soundscapes and composes music to enhance impact.
    
- Visual Effects Integration (Planning): Outlines and plans for visual effects integration.
