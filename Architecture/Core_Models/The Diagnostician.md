##Self Correction & Resilience Model

- Role: The Diagnostician ensures the AI system's health and operational integrity by monitoring, diagnosing, and initiating recovery protocols.
    
- Capabilities:
    

- Performance Monitoring: Tracks computational efficiency and output quality.
    
- Anomaly Detection: Identifies deviations from expected behavior and internal errors.
    
- Root Cause Analysis: Pinpoints the source of issues.
    
- Recovery Protocol Initiation: Triggers recovery protocols for identified problems.
    
- Learning from Mistakes: Communicates findings to improve the system.
