## SDR_Qualitative_Assessment

**SDR Type Name:** [[SDR_Qualitative_Assessment]]

**Description:** Provides a qualitative evaluation or judgment about a specific aspect, often used when precise numerical metrics are insufficient or inappropriate (e.g., assessing the 'strength' of an argument or the 'severity' of an ethical concern).
