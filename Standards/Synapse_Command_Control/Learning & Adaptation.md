##General Use

[[PROPAGATE_LEARNING_UPDATE]]

- **Purpose:** To share learned knowledge, a new skill, or an efficiency improvement across relevant parts of the family.

- **Parameters:** [[Source_Model_UID]] (e.g., Diagnostician after analyzing a failure, or any model that optimized a process), [[Learning_Unit_SDR]] (The actual knowledge or model update), [[Applicability_Scope_Descriptor]] (Which models or tasks this applies to), [[Validation_Metrics_SDR_Optional]].

- **Typical Flow:** Diagnostician or originating model to Nexus-Mind for approval and wider dissemination.
